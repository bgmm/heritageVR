VR Puzzle
for Digital Heritage

Bring the pieces of the history together!
It is a research project based on shape representation studies in virtual reality environment.

Learn more

Problem

The shape representation mediums and tools that are available today are mostly based on 2D representation techniques, although the shapes we perceive and interact are in 3D.

Goal

This study aims to contribute to the use of 3D modeling & visualization tools in architectural education in the context of understanding destroyed or ruined heritage in shape representation studies.

Approach

The 3D puzzle method supports the visual-spatial model by allowing participants to perceive the shapes each time through their contours. Even though the model pieces are equipped with regional information, participants need to detect and match the contours to put together the pieces.

Dark Church in Cappadocia

Since the participants have less information about the cave monasteries and churches of Cappadocia as well as The Dark Church, the unfamiliar geometry and topology enable participants to see the shapes and shape relations in a creative way.

A research project @Istanbul Technical University
