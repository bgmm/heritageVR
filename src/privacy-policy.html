This privacy policy (this "Policy") describes how Pasilobus Inc. and its affiliates and related companies (collectively, "Pasilobus", "Company", "us", "our", and "we") collect, use, and share personal information of users of the www.pasilobus.com website (the "Site"), and/or any application, service, functionality, experience, event, or product belonging to, provided by, created by, or operated by Pasilobus Inc. (collectively or individually, the "Service").

What We Collect

We get information about you in a range of ways.

Information You Give Us. We may collect your‎ name, email address, phone number, username, password, demographic information (such as your gender and occupation) and any other information you may provide directly on our Service.

Information We Get From Others. We may get information about you from other sources. We may add this to information we get from this Service.

Information Automatically Collected. We automatically log information about you and your computer and mobile device when you use our Service. For example, when visiting our Service, we may log browser type, browser language, the website you visited before browsing to our Service, pages you viewed, how long you spent on a page, access times and information about your use of and actions on our Service.

Cookies. We may log information using "Cookies". Cookies are small data files stored on your hard drive by a website. We may use both session Cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and persistent Cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them) to provide you with a more personal and interactive experience on our Service. This type of information is collected to make the Service more useful to you and to tailor the experience with us to meet your special interests and needs. For more information about cookies, and how to disable cookies, visit http://www.allaboutcookies.org. "Log files" track actions occurring on the Site, and collect data including your IP address, browser type, Internet service provider, referring/exit pages, and date/time stamps. “Web beacons,” “tags,” and “pixels” are electronic files used to record information about how you browse the Site.

Location Information

We may use and store information about your location depending on the permissions you have set on your device. We use this information to provide features of our Service, and to improve and customize our Service. You can enable or disable location services when you use our Service at any time, through your mobile device settings.

operate, maintain, and improve the Service, products, and other services;

send information including confirmations, technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support and administrative messages;

communicate about promotions, upcoming events, educational materials, and news about products and services offered by us and our partners;

enable you to send information, materials, and documents to friends, partners, and other Users;

personalize your experience on our Service by informing you of events, offers, promotions, and opportunities that may interest you; and

offer, deliver, or make available products and services that customers request.

We may share personal information:

with our third-party payment processor, in order to process and execute payment transactions approved and/or initiated by you;

with third parties subject to your consent. For example, you may choose to allow us to share personal information with third-parties for their own marketing purposes. Such uses will be subject to each third party’s respective privacy policies;

with third parties when we negotiate or execute a business deal involving the sale or transfer of all or a part of our business or assets. These deals can include any merger, financing, acquisition, or bankruptcy transaction or proceeding;

for legal, protection, and safety purposes, including to:

a

comply with federal, state, and local laws and regulations;

b

protect the rights and property of Pasilobus Inc., our agents, customers, and others. This includes enforcing our agreements, policies, and terms of use; and share information in an emergency. This includes protecting the safety of our employees and agents, our customers, or any person.

with our employees and third party service providers to enable them to provide services such as email delivery, text messaging, customer support, voice messaging, auditing, website hosting, information technology, and other services.

We may also share aggregated and/or anonymized data with others for their own uses. We may disclose Personal Information as we in our sole discretion deem to be necessary or appropriate: (a) to comply with legal processes or proceedings; (b) under applicable law, which may include laws outside the jurisdiction where you reside and/or conduct your business; (c) to protect the operations of Company; (d) to respond to requests or orders from public and government authorities, which may include public and government authorities outside the jurisdiction where you reside and/or conduct your business; (e) to enforce Company’s Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, or other terms and conditions; (f) to enable Company to pursue remedies or limit the damages or injuries that we may incur, and (g) to protect rights, privacy, safety, or property of you, other Users, Company, or other parties.

Our marketing emails tell you how to "opt-out." If you opt out, we may still send you non-marketing emails. Non-marketing emails include emails about your accounts and our business dealings with you.

You may send requests about personal information to our Contact Information below. You can request to change contact choices, opt-out of our sharing with others, and update your personal information.

You can typically remove and reject cookies from the Service with your browser settings. Many browsers are set to accept cookies until you change your settings. If you remove or reject our cookies, it could affect how the Service works for you.

Only persons aged 18 or older have permission to access our Service. Any and all persons under the age of 18 (“Minors”) must obtain authorization and consent from a parent or legal guardian prior to accessing the Service.

The Service does not address anyone under the age of 13 (each a “Child” and collectively, “Children”) without the authorization, consent, and active participation of such child’s parent(s) or legal guardian(s).

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from Children. If you are a parent or guardian and you learn that your Child has provided us with Personal Information without your authorization and consent, please contact us directly. If we become aware that we have collected Personal Information from a Child or a Minor without verification of consent from a parent or legal guardian, we may attempt to remove that information from our servers.
